Coraline is a dark fantasy horror film about an adventurous girl. It was based on the novel made by Neil Gaiman in 2002. Coraline discovered a secret door in the home she just moved into. The world behind that door seemed almost perfect, it's as if everything she ever wanted was behind it. This happiness lasted until she found out about the truth. This movie is popular due to the hidden meanings and foreshadowing throughout it. Until now, fans are still creating and discovering new theories.


Coraline's family:

  • Melinda Jones (mother)
  • Charlie Jones (father)
  • Coraline (daughter)
